📖 How It Works – Sequence

1
Fill in transaction details – Set Merchant ID, amount, payment option, and your unique Transaction ID (must be unique per order).
2
Generate Hash – The hash is HMAC-SHA512 of concatenated fields signed with your Public Key, then Base64-encoded. This prevents tampering.
3
POST to PayWay – Send all fields + hash as multipart/form-data from a whitelisted domain.
4
Render checkout – For cards: embed the returned checkout URL in an <iframe>. For ABA Pay: show the QR string.
5
Receive pushback – After payment, PayWay POSTs a notification to your return_url with transaction status, bank ref, and amount.
